FAQ

Questions answered

What is n8n?
n8n is a free, open-source workflow automation tool — like Zapier, but self-hostable and more powerful. Tooldexo generates blueprints you import directly. Use n8n Cloud (free trial) or self-host for free.
Do I need technical knowledge?
None. Describe your workflow in plain English, download the blueprint, and import it into your platform of choice. You just connect your app credentials and activate.
What counts as a "parse"?
Each time you describe a workflow and Claude maps it into a structured blueprint, that's one parse. Free gives you 5/month. Pro is unlimited.
Which integrations are supported?
30+ integrations mapped — Slack, Gmail, HubSpot, Airtable, Notion, Stripe, Typeform, GitHub, Trello, Google Sheets, Asana, and more. All correctly typed for each export platform.
Can I cancel Pro anytime?
Yes — cancel anytime from inside the app. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. No fees, no friction.
Is my data private?
Your workflow descriptions are processed by Claude (Anthropic) to generate blueprints. We store only your email and usage count. Your data is never sold.
