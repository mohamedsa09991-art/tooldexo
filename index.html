04 — FAQ

Common questions.

What is n8n?
n8n is a free, open-source workflow automation tool — like Zapier or Make, but self-hostable and significantly more powerful. Tooldexo generates blueprints you import directly into n8n. You can use n8n Cloud (free trial available) or self-host at no cost.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
None. You describe your workflow in plain English. Tooldexo and Claude do the technical heavy lifting. After importing the blueprint into n8n, you connect your accounts (Gmail, Slack, etc.) through simple credential forms — no code, no configuration files.
What counts as a "parse"?
Each time you describe a workflow and Claude maps it into a structured blueprint, that counts as one parse. The free plan includes 5 per month. Pro is unlimited — parse as many workflows as your operation demands.
Which integrations are supported?
Tooldexo maps 30+ integrations to their correct n8n node types — including Slack, Gmail, HubSpot, Airtable, Notion, Stripe, Typeform, GitHub, Trello, Google Sheets, and more. If n8n supports it, we can map it.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
Yes — cancel anytime directly from within the app. You retain Pro access through the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no retention calls, no friction.
How is my data handled?
Your workflow descriptions are processed by Claude (Anthropic) to generate blueprints, subject to Anthropic's privacy standards. We store only your email address and usage count. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.
