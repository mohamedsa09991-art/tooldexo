FAQ

Questions answered

What is n8n?
n8n is a free, open-source workflow automation tool — like Zapier or Make, but self-hostable and more powerful. Tooldexo generates blueprints you import directly into n8n. You can use n8n Cloud (free trial) or self-host it for free.
Do I need to know how to code?
Not at all. You describe your workflow in plain English — Tooldexo and Claude do the rest. After importing the blueprint into n8n, you just add your API credentials (like connecting your Gmail or Slack account) and activate.
What is a "parse"?
Each time you describe a workflow and Claude maps it into a structured blueprint, that counts as one parse. The free plan gives you 5 per month. Pro is unlimited.
Which tools and apps does it support?
Tooldexo maps 30+ integrations to their correct n8n node types — including Slack, Gmail, HubSpot, Airtable, Notion, Stripe, Typeform, GitHub, Trello, and more. If n8n supports it, we can map it.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
Yes — cancel anytime from inside the app. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no awkward retention flow.
Is my data private?
Your workflow descriptions are sent to Claude (Anthropic) to generate the blueprint — the same privacy standards as Claude.ai. We store your email and usage count. We never sell your data.
